Carrot cake: Brazilian vs American version

introduction

brazilian carrot cake recipe

The brazilan carrot cake is know for a strong orange color, really soft dough, and the fact that the carrort is involved in the dough

Ingredients

  • 3 medium carrots
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 2 cups of wheat flour
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder

How to make it

1

  • In a blender, place 3 medium carrots, 3 eggs, 1 cup of canola oil and 2 cups of sugar. Blend until smooth.
  • 2 In a bowl, place 2 cups of wheat flour, 1 pinch of salt and 1 tablespoon of chemical yeast. Mix.
  • 3 Then add the blender mixture to the bowl.
  • 4 Using a whisk, mix until smooth.
  • 5 Transfer the dough to a greased and floured pan.
  • 6 Bake in a preheated oven at 180 degrees Celsius for 40 minutes.

Brasilian carrot cake recipe

The best carrot cake recipe that you can make in your life!

The brazilan carrot cake has its origins in tradicional home baking, influenced by European recipes, but adapted to local ingredients. Unlike the american version, which includes grated carrots for texture, the Brazilian carrot cake is made by blending carrots into the batter, giving it a smooth, vibrant orange color. It is typically described as soft, moist, and slight sweet, with a delicate carrot flavour, and a perfect combination with chocolate glaze. loved for its simplicity and ease of preparation!

Ingredients

  • 3 medium carrots
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 2 cups of wheat flour
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 cup of oil
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder

How to make it

  • In a blender, place 3 medium carrots, 3 eggs, 1 cup of vegetal oil and 1/2 cups of sugar. Blend until smooth.
  • In a bowl, place 2 cups of wheat flour, 1 pinch of salt and 1 tablespoon of chemical yeast. Mix.
  • Then add the blender mixture to the bowl.
  • Using a whisk, mix until smooth.
  • Transfer the dough to a greased and floured pan.
  • Bake in a preheated oven at 180 degrees Celsius for 40 minutes.

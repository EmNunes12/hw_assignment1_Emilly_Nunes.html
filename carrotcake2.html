Brazilian Carrot Cake Recipe

The Best Carrot Cake Recipe You Can Make in Your Life!

The Brazilian carrot cake has its origins in traditional home baking, influenced by European recipes but adapted to local ingredients. Unlike the American version, which includes grated carrots for texture, the Brazilian carrot cake is made by blending carrots into the batter, giving it a smooth, vibrant orange color. It is typically described as soft, moist, and slightly sweet, with a delicate carrot flavor. It pairs perfectly with chocolate glaze and is loved for its simplicity and ease of preparation!

Ingredients

  • 3 medium carrots
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 2 cups of wheat flour
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 cup of oil
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder

How to Make It

  • In a blender, place 3 medium carrots, 3 eggs, 1 cup of vegetable oil, and 1/2 cup of sugar. Blend until smooth.
  • In a bowl, place 2 cups of wheat flour, 1 pinch of salt, and 1 tablespoon of baking powder. Mix well.
  • Then, add the blender mixture to the bowl.
  • Using a whisk, mix until smooth.
  • Transfer the batter to a greased and floured pan.
  • Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C (350°F) for 40 minutes.
